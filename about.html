About Monolit

Monolit started with a boy with a strong passion towards candles who wants all Malaysians to experience and aware of the effect of an aromatherapy candles do to their body, mind and soul.

Monolit cares to an extend of producing toxic-free candles by using beeswax as they neutralize your home by adding a sweet aroma to your home and have a longer burning time compared to paraffin wax candles.

Monolit uses the best quality of scented candles for a more present smell for Malaysians. Currently, Monolit plans to release more various scent in the future as we are atill studying the market.
